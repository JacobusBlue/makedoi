You are killing the sun
Stop buying solar panels

Reported by a researcher from the national institute of solar research

Our sun is dying! Reports Piet Grap Gouws a researcher from the national institute of solar research. Gouws said to SABC NEWS that the increased inamount of solar panels being put up in South Africa, due to loadshedding, led to Solar Dimming. Solar Dimming as explained to SABC NEWS is a phenomenon that occurs when the sun is being over used.

The sun is like any other resource we have on earth. If we don't take care of it we will lose it ~Gouws
